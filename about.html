The Past Journey

Our major service is Shiba Inu 101. This is an email newsletter that aims to educate all Shiba lovers on everything they should know about Shiba Inu. The service is free during three months following the subscription, then charged at a very afforardable price afterwards. To know more about our pricing policy, click HERE.

Our headquarter is located at Seattle, United States. But our service is delivered to all over the world. Since the official launch in late 2018, our customer base has been growing exponentially each year. As of 2022, we have already accumulated over 2 million subscriptions

We always place the customer value at our priority. Therefore, Shiba Inu 101 offers highly customizable contents. More importantly, we have one of the best email feeback system in the North America. We are open to any positive or negative suggestions from our customers, so that we could do better to tailor their needs.

Meet the Founder

Hi, my name is Ke Zhou, the founder of SHIBU INA.

You might notice that we spelled Shiba Inu wrong as Shibu Ina. Well, that was really a mispelling at first, but then our team members started to fell in love with such an illusional contrast. So we decided to use that as our company's name. What a beautiful mistake, isn't it?

To be honest, the company was first initiated as nothing but a university project. It was not until the first subscription email have we realized that this whole idea could become a real thing! Therefore, my crew and I worked days and nights building our product, network, and customer base.

Finally, as of 2022, we have accumulated more than 2 million subscriotions. Meanwhile, we have recieved our first round of financing from Shiba Token. In the near future, we will spare no efforts to come up with more functionalities for our product, so as to provide a better experience for all our beloved customers.
